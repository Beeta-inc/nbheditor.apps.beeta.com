# NbhEditor UI Features Guide

## 🌟 Glass Mode

### What is Glass Mode?
Glass Mode gives NbhEditor a modern, semi-transparent aesthetic with dark backgrounds and glowing accents. It's optimized to work smoothly on all Android devices from version 7.0 and up.

### How to Use
1. Open the home screen (tap the hamburger menu → Home)
2. Look for the "Glass" button at the top
3. Tap to toggle between normal and glass mode
4. The button will show "✦ Glass ON" when active

### Features
- Semi-transparent dark panels with white borders
- Enhanced text readability with shadows
- Smooth transition animations
- Works perfectly on older devices

## 🎤 Voice Input

### Getting Started
1. Tap the microphone icon (🎤) in the editor or AI chat
2. Grant microphone permission if prompted
3. Wait for "Speak now" notification
4. Start speaking clearly

### Visual Feedback
- **Blue mic**: Ready to use
- **Orange pulsing mic**: Actively listening
- **Hint text**: Shows "🎤 Listening..." and partial results
- **Success**: Shows "✓ Voice inserted" when done

### Tips for Best Results
- Speak clearly and at normal pace
- Wait for the "Speak now" notification
- The app will automatically add spaces between words
- Works offline on most devices (some may require internet)

### Troubleshooting
- **"No speech detected"**: Speak louder or closer to mic
- **"Timed out"**: Tap mic again and speak immediately
- **"Mic busy"**: Wait a moment and try again
- **"Voice needs network"**: Some devices require internet connection
